### Priority Recommendations (groups that need the most attention)

1. **Adductors (1 -> 6):** Most underrepresented group. Only has a machine exercise. Adding bodyweight, cable, and free weight options is critical for complete lower body training.
2. **Traps (4 -> 9):** Missing lower/mid trap work entirely (Y-Raise, overhead shrug). Important for shoulder health and balanced upper back development.
3. **Lats (3 -> 7):** Only has 3 standard pulldown variations. Adding grip/stance variations and unilateral options provides much better lat training variety.
4. **Triceps (10 -> 14):** Missing the DB kickback (one of the highest EMG-activation tricep exercises per ACE research) and the reverse grip pushdown.
5. **Back (22 -> 27):** Well-covered numerically but missing popular named variations (Pendlay, Meadows, Seal Row) that are gym staples.
